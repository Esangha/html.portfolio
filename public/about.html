About Me

Possible Ajing Esangha is a graduate of mass communication, pianist, bassist, acoustic guitarist and a worship leader.

He hails from yala local government area of Cross River state. Born on the 8th February 1996 to Rev/Mrs Victor Esangha both from Ukelle, in yala local government area of Cross River state.

He is a prolific poet and an advocate of equality among all races and genders.

He is the author of what if I fail, dazed the enslaved mind, Love fantasy, My saviour, the slave master among other poems he writes during his spare time.
